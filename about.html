Our Story

Where We Started

Eighteen years of real craftsmanship. A reputation for quality, clean job sites, and a team that genuinely cares.

The Company Before The Brand

A name with heritage, waiting to be told

For eighteen years, Nordic Built has done the work quietly: clean job sites, honest estimates, and cabinetry that holds up long after the truck leaves the driveway. But the brand itself never caught up to the company Jon David actually built.

There was no visual identity — a name with deep heritage potential, but no mark, colors, or system to carry it. The story of faith, generosity, and team investment was mentioned in passing, but never really felt. "Nordic" was a name, not yet a story. The craft tradition behind it was untapped.

The Heritage We're Reclaiming

Ordinary craftsmen, called upon for extraordinary work

The Nordic peoples carried a fearsome reputation — but most were ordinary farmers and craftsmen first. Calm and humble until called upon. Fiercely committed to their families, their land, and each other. Strength in service of others, not power for its own sake. That is the truth this brand is built on.

The Mark

An original rune, cut like a timber joint

Our mark is an original rune-inspired symbol — never copied from an actual historic rune. It doubles as an N monogram: two joined verticals, carved rather than drawn, built the way a craftsman would cut a single timber joint.

It's a small detail, but it's the same principle behind everything we build: nothing borrowed, nothing rushed, made to hold together for a long time.
